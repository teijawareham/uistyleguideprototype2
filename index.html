UI Style Guide
Prototype
by: Teija Wareham

The goal of a UI style guide is to provide a comprehensive set of design guidelines and standards that ensures consistency. By establishing clear rules for elements like colors, fonts, and layout, a style guide helps maintain a cohesive look and feel throughout a project. This not only enhances the user experience but also streamlines the design process, making it easier for teams to collaborate and create visually appealing interfaces.

The Logo & Branding

Our logo is the face of our brand, representing who we are and what we stand for. To maintain a strong and consistent brand identity, it's important to follow these logo rules and dimensions:

Logo Usage:

Desktop Dimensions: h: 40px x w: 120px
Ensure the logo is displayed prominently and clearly visible.
Avoid distorting, rotating, or altering the logo in any way.
Always use the approved version of the logo.

We embrace minimalistic iconography to enhance user experience on our website. The Display Card

Our display cards are designed with simplicity in mind, presenting products and descriptions in a clear and concise manner. Each card showcases the product prominently, accompanied by a brief yet informative description that highlights its key features. With clean layouts and minimal distractions, our cards prioritize clarity, making it easy for users to quickly assess the product and make informed decisions.
